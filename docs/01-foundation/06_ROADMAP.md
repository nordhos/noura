# NOURA Roadmap

Version: 1.0

Status: Living Document

---

# Purpose

The roadmap defines the long-term evolution of NOURA.

It is not a list of features.

It is a sequence of capabilities that gradually increase users' Financial Clarity.

Each phase builds upon the previous one.

Features should only be introduced when the foundation of the previous phase is complete.

---

# Phase 1

## Financial Foundation

### Goal

Establish a reliable financial system.

### Objective

Enable users to create a trusted foundation for managing their finances.

### Capabilities

- Financial Setup
- Financial Timeline
- Profile Management
- Transaction Categories
- Initial Balance
- Income Recording
- Expense Recording
- Transaction History
- Dashboard
- Basic Reports

### Success Criteria

Users can accurately record their financial activities and understand their current financial condition.

---

# Phase 2

## Financial Understanding

### Goal

Help users understand their financial behavior.

### Objective

Transform raw transactions into meaningful insights.

### Capabilities

- Monthly Reports
- Category Analysis
- Income Analysis
- Expense Analysis
- Spending Trends
- Financial Health
- Smart Insights

### Success Criteria

Users understand where their money comes from and where it goes.

---

# Phase 3

## Financial Guidance

### Goal

Support better financial decisions.

### Objective

Guide users through personalized recommendations.

### Capabilities

- Budget Recommendations
- Spending Warnings
- Savings Opportunities
- Recurring Transaction Detection
- Cash Flow Alerts
- Personalized Suggestions

### Success Criteria

Users make better financial decisions based on data.

---

# Phase 4

## Financial Planning

### Goal

Prepare users for future financial goals.

### Objective

Shift focus from recording the past to planning the future.

### Capabilities

- Savings Goals
- Emergency Fund Tracker
- Debt Planning
- Financial Targets
- Future Cash Flow Projection
- Goal Progress Tracking

### Success Criteria

Users actively plan their financial future.

---

# Phase 5

## Financial Intelligence

### Goal

Provide proactive financial assistance.

### Objective

Enable NOURA to become an intelligent financial companion.

### Capabilities

- AI Financial Assistant
- Predictive Cash Flow
- Spending Forecast
- Financial Risk Detection
- Personalized Financial Coaching
- Scenario Simulation

### Success Criteria

NOURA proactively helps users make confident financial decisions.

---

# Roadmap Principles

The roadmap is guided by the following priorities.

1. Financial Clarity over Feature Quantity

Every new capability must improve Financial Clarity.

---

2. Foundation before Expansion

A strong foundation is always more valuable than adding new features.

---

3. Simplicity before Automation

Users must first understand their finances before automation is introduced.

---

4. Guidance before Intelligence

Recommendations should come before prediction.

Prediction should come before automation.

---

5. Long-Term Consistency

Every new phase should feel like a natural evolution of the previous one.
