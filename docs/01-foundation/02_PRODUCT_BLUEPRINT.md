# Product Identity

Product Name

NOURA

Category

Financial Clarity System

---

# Product Vision

Help individuals and couples understand their financial condition with clarity, confidence, and consistency.

NOURA exists to reduce uncertainty about money.

Every feature should move users closer to understanding their financial condition.

---

# Product Mission

Transform financial activities into financial clarity.

Financial clarity creates confidence.

Confidence enables better financial decisions.

---

# Target Users

NOURA is intentionally built for only two scenarios.

## Individual

One person managing personal finances.

---

## Couple

A husband and wife managing household finances together.

---

Supporting more than two profiles is intentionally outside the scope of NOURA.

This focus allows the product to remain simple, opinionated, and deeply optimized for its target users.

---

# Product Principles

## 1. Financial Clarity First

Every feature must improve users' understanding of their financial condition.

---

## 2. Simplicity Over Complexity

Every screen should be easy to understand.

Every workflow should require the fewest possible decisions.

---

## 3. Confidence Through Consistency

Financial information must always be calculated using consistent rules.

Users should never question where numbers come from.

---

## 4. Trust Before Features

New features should never reduce trust.

Accuracy is always more important than feature quantity.

---

## 5. Progressive Experience

NOURA should grow with users.

The first experience should be simple.

Advanced understanding comes naturally over time.

---

# Product Scope

NOURA focuses on personal financial clarity.

The product intentionally does not attempt to become:

- Accounting software
- ERP
- Business finance software
- Investment platform
- Banking application
- Budget collaboration platform

These decisions are intentional.

Focus creates clarity.

# User Promise

Whenever users open NOURA, they should be able to answer five questions immediately.

1.

How much money do I have?

---

2.

Where did my money come from?

---

3.

Where did my money go?

---

4.

Am I doing better than before?

---

5.

What should I pay attention to next?

If NOURA successfully answers these five questions, then it has fulfilled its purpose.
